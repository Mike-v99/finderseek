The process

How it works

Four steps from signup to a city full of seekers chasing your treasure.

Step 01
📋
Apply & Get Approved
Fill out the form below. We review your city and approve Pirates on a rolling basis — one or two per city to start.
Step 02
💰
Set Your Prize
Choose your prize amount (minimum $20). Write your clues, pick your hiding spot, and set your hunt live date.
Step 03
✉️
Hide the Envelope
We send you a sealed envelope with a unique 6-digit code inside. You hide it somewhere in your city based on your clues.
Step 04
🏆
Seeker Claims It
When a seeker finds the envelope and enters the code, the hunt closes. You pay out the prize — your identity stays hidden the whole time.
Prize transfer

When the seeker claims the code, you receive a notification and pay them directly via Zelle or Venmo. The hunt closes automatically with a "Treasure Found" banner.
  • No upfront deposit needed
  • Direct between Pirate & Seeker
  • Pirate identity shared with seeker
  • Requires manual payout
  • Lower seeker confidence
The pirate code

Rules of the hunt

Every Pirate agrees to these rules when they apply.

1
The prize must be real. Minimum prize is $20. You must be able to pay out within 48 hours of a seeker claiming the code.
2
Hide it safely. Envelopes must be hidden in publicly accessible locations. No private property, no dangerous locations, no places that require trespassing.
3
Clues must be fair. Your clues must genuinely lead to the hiding spot. No impossible riddles — challenging is good, unsolvable is not.
4
One active hunt at a time. Each Pirate can only have one live hunt per city per week. Seekers choose one hunt — make yours worth finding.
5
If no one finds it, you retrieve it. If the hunt ends unclaimed, you retrieve the envelope and may re-hide or roll the prize over to the next week.
