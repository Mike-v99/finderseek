Questions
How does early clue access work? ▼
Free users see clues as they drop on their normal schedule. Gold Seekers see the next unrevealed clue one step ahead — giving you a head start on finding the treasure.
What does a promoted hunt look like? ▼
When you're a Gold Pirate and your hunt is active, it appears pinned at the top of the hunt listings with a ✦ FEATURED banner. More eyes = faster finds = faster prize delivery.
Can I cancel anytime? ▼
Yes — cancel anytime from your profile page. You keep Gold perks until the end of your current billing period, then revert to the free Explorer tier.
Do I need Gold to hide or find treasure? ▼
Nope! The free tier gives you full access to hide treasure, hunt for prizes, and claim wins. Gold just gives you a competitive edge and helps support the platform.
Is my payment secure? ▼
Payments are processed by Stripe — we never see or store your card details. FinderSeek uses Stripe Checkout, the same infrastructure trusted by millions of businesses.
