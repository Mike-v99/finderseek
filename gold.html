How does early clue access work? ▾
Free users see clues as they drop on schedule. Gold Seekers see the next unrevealed clue one step ahead — giving you a real head start on finding the treasure.
What does a promoted quest look like? ▾
When you're Gold and your quest is active, it appears pinned at the top of quest listings with a ✦ FEATURED banner. More eyes means faster finds.
How does the free trial work? ▾
You get full Gold access for 30 days completely free. After that, it's $3.99/mo. You can cancel anytime before the trial ends and you won't be charged.
Can I cancel anytime? ▾
Yes — cancel anytime from your profile. You keep Gold perks until the end of your billing period, then revert to free.
Do I need Gold to play? ▾
Nope! Free gives you full access to hide treasure, find prizes, and claim wins. Gold just gives you a competitive edge and supports the platform.
