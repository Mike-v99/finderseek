QUESTIONS
What is the finder clue alert? ▾
Every quest has one special ⚡ finder clue hidden randomly in the sequence. It contains enough detail to go find the prize right now. Gold members get an instant push notification the moment it drops so they can move first.
What does editing AI clues mean? ▾
When you create a quest, AI writes your clues. Gold Quest Masters can edit any clue text after it's generated — tweak the wording, add local flavor, or adjust difficulty before the quest goes live.
How does the free trial work? ▾
Full Gold access free for 30 days. After that it's $3.99/mo. Cancel anytime before the trial ends and you won't be charged.
Can I cancel anytime? ▾
Yes — cancel from your profile at any time. You keep Gold perks until the end of your billing period, then revert to free with no data lost.
Do I need Gold to play? ▾
Nope. Free gives full access to seek treasure, create quests, and claim prizes. Gold gives you a competitive edge and helps support FinderSeek.
