How It Works

Six clues.
One prize.
Seven days.

Every Friday, a real cash prize is hidden somewhere in your city. The first clue drops at 8 AM sharp.

A new clue unlocks every morning at 8 AM. Free members get Clues 1–4. Pro members get all six plus a photo clue.
