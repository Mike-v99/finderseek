The adventure

How it works.

Real cash. Hidden in your city. A new treasure drops every Friday. Here's everything you need to know to find it.

Step 01
A Pirate hides the treasure
Every week, a local Pirate — someone in your city — hides a sealed envelope somewhere publicly accessible. Inside is a unique 6-digit claim code and the prize amount. The location is their secret. Your job is to find it.

Step 02
Browse active hunts in your city
Open FinderSeek and see all live treasure hunts near you. Each hunt shows the prize amount, how many clues have dropped, and how many days remain. Pick one — but choose wisely. Seekers can only join one hunt per week.
Step 03
Follow the daily clue drops
Starting Friday at 8 AM, a new clue drops every day. Free members get the first 4 clues. Pro members unlock all 6 clues plus a photo clue on Thursday — a massive advantage. Each clue gets more specific. The sooner you find it, the bigger the bragging rights.

Step 04
Find the envelope & enter the code
You found it! Open the envelope and grab the 6-digit code inside. Enter it on FinderSeek to officially claim the treasure. The hunt closes instantly — the prize is yours. No one else can claim it.
Step 05
Claim your prize
Once you enter the winning code, your prize is confirmed. Payment is sent via Zelle or Venmo — your choice. If the Pirate used the FinderSeek escrow option, payment arrives within minutes. Honor system Pirates have 48 hours to pay out.
