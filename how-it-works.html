What makes a good hiding spot?▼
Public, safe, accessible — park bench, under a rock, near a landmark. No private property.
How do I protect the code from weather?▼
Use a zip-lock bag or laminated card. Just keep it readable until found.
Can I edit clues before going live?▼
Yes — review and regenerate after AI creates them. Once live, clues are locked.
What if nobody finds it?▼
Hunt expires, you keep everything. Go retrieve your code and re-hide or keep the prize.
