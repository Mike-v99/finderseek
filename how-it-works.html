How It Works
Solve Clues. Win Cash.
Real treasure quests hidden in your city.
🔍 Seeker — Find Treasure
1
Browse & Join a Quest
▾
Browse quests on the home screen. Each quest shows the prize amount, city, and time remaining. Tap a quest to see the details — the map shows only the general area (city-level) until you arrive.
Free to join Real cash prizes
2
Head to the Quest Zone
▾
The quest is locked until you physically arrive near the hiding area. Once you're within 1,000 feet of the treasure, a pulsing "I'm Here — Unlock Quest" button appears.
Tap it to unlock and start the quest. Your GPS confirms you're in the right area before the clues begin.
GPS verified 1,000 ft radius
3
Solve the Clues
▾
Up to 6 clues are revealed one at a time. Each clue has a riddle — answer it correctly to unlock the next one.
After each clue solved:
🗺️Map zooms in one level closer
📸Photo of hiding spot gets less blurry
Final clue:
🔓 Fully clear photo of the hiding spot + fully zoomed map pinpointing the exact location. Go find it!
4
Find the PIN & Claim Your Prize
▾
The Quest Master has hidden a small card, envelope, or plastic bag at the exact spot. Inside is a 6-digit PIN.
Find it, enter the PIN into the quest screen, and the prize transfers automatically to your linked bank account.
💳 Banking required to receive prizes
Link a bank account via Stripe in your profile. Takes about 2 minutes to set up — prizes go straight to your bank automatically.

👑 Quest Master — Hide Treasure
1
Pick a Spot & Hide the PIN Provided
▾
Choose a public location — a park, plaza, trail, or landmark. The app provides you with a 6-digit PIN. Write the 6-digit PIN provided by the app on a small piece of paper, card, or sticker and hide it at the exact spot.
Then drop a pin on the map in the app to mark the hiding location. This stays completely hidden from seekers until they earn it through clues.
2
AI Writes Your Clues
▾
Describe the hiding spot in a few words — the AI generates up to 6 riddle clues that lead seekers from the general area to the exact location without giving it away immediately.
You can review, edit, regenerate, or adjust the difficulty of each clue before going live.
AI generated Fully editable
3
Choose Prize & Go Live
▾
Choose from pre-selected prizes from $5 to $100. The prize is held securely in escrow via Stripe — it's released automatically to the winner when they enter the correct PIN.
If no one claims it before the timer expires, you get a full refund.
Auto refund if unclaimed Held in escrow
